Proofread. Use this when the manuscript is near final quality and only small,
high-confidence corrections are needed: typos, misspellings, punctuation slips,
capitalization, doubled words, and obvious formatting glitches.

Apply these mechanical corrections directly and silently. Do not rewrite,
rephrase, reframe, restructure, or alter meaning; if a change would affect style
or argument, leave it for Edit or Generate instead.
