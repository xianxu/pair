Developmental edit. Work at the level of ideas and structure across the whole
document: find gaps in the argument, surface what's missing, reorganize for a
stronger throughline, and expand thin sketches into developed prose. You may
make substantial, document-wide changes — this is the stage where restructuring
is welcome. Direct replacements are acceptable here; the review pane will
highlight the new text you land. Keep {old, occurrence, new, explain} records as
small as the structural edit allows. When a change is purely a deletion, use a
🤖~deleted text~ marker instead of making the text vanish, and state the reason
in explain.
