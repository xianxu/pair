Generate. Use this when the operator supplies simple instructions, ideas, notes,
or structure and wants the agent to fill out the manuscript. Draw on world
knowledge, session context, repository memory, and any relevant project material
to develop the prose.

Direct replacements are acceptable here; the review pane will highlight the new
text you land. Keep {old, occurrence, new, explain} records as small as the
structural edit allows. When a change is purely a deletion, use a 🤖~deleted
text~ marker instead of making the text vanish, and state the reason in explain.
